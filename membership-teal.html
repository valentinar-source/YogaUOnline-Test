Questions?

Frequently asked questions

When does enrollment open?

We open the doors twice a year — the next enrollment window opens in September 2026. Waitlist members are notified first and receive early-bird pricing.

How much does the membership cost?

Membership pricing is announced when enrollment opens. Waitlist members always receive the best available rate. [Placeholder — confirm pricing display strategy with Eva.]

I'm a member but I have two logins — which one do I use?

Your Membership account covers live classes, the Practice Channel, and Yoga Lab. If you've also purchased individual courses, those live in our Course Library with a separate password (same email). See the login guide above, or contact us and we'll sort it out.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time — no phone calls, no hoops. You'll keep access through the end of your billing period.

Is this right for me if I'm a beginner? Or over 50? Or a teacher?

Yes, yes, and yes. Our practices are designed to be tailored to your body — with options by level, length, and focus. Many members are over 50, many are teachers, and many are both.
